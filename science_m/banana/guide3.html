Learning Objective

Observe the fruit-making magic God designed.

1. For Preschoolers

Food does not just get born in the store.

God prepared a magical mechanism so that bananas grow on trees.

From nothing, a tiny fruit appears and slowly grows.

Each child has the chance to watch this process with their own eyes.

2. For Elementary School Children

Why is it that bananas don’t grow in Massachusetts, yet here, banana trees bear fruit so abundantly on their own?

3. For Middle School Students

Banana fruits are green at first. Why do they turn yellow as they mature?

Advanced Study

  • Why does the sweetness of a banana increase after it is harvested and left to sit?
  • No sugar is injected after harvest, so why does the banana become sweeter on its own?
  • What is the most appropriate time to harvest bananas?
  • Bananas from the Philippines are harvested while still green and shipped by boat for import. Should bananas here also be harvested while still green?

For Graduate School Level

  • No high-tech laboratory can replicate the miraculous sugar synthesis method by which bananas become sweeter just by being left alone.
  • Witness this wondrous magic prepared by God.

For Adults

  • Is eating a banana right after it turns yellow the best way to enjoy it?
  • Why doesn’t the banana tree make the fruit sweet from the beginning?
  • What are the raw materials that create the sweetness in bananas?
